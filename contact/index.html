Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you typically work on?

I specialize in full-stack web applications, quantitative finance models, data analytics dashboards, and custom software solutions. My projects range from simple websites to complex financial modeling systems.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, I offer various support packages including bug fixes, updates, maintenance, and feature additions. We can discuss ongoing support options during project planning.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer fixed-price packages for standard projects and custom pricing for complex solutions. All projects include detailed proposals with transparent pricing breakdowns.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am experienced in remote collaboration. I'm flexible with time zones and communication preferences.

Can you help with existing projects or only new development?

I can help with both! Whether you need to improve an existing system, add new features, or start from scratch, I'm happy to assist. I can also help with code reviews and optimization.
